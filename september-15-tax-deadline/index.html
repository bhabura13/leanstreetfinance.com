Don’t Forget: September 15 is the Quarterly IRS Tax Deadline!

/ Newsletter / By Lean Street Finance, LLC

September 15 is the deadline for Q3 federal estimated tax payments. Not paying or underpaying can result in unnecessary penalties.

Do you know if you are paying the right amount?

  • Too little = IRS penalties
  • Too much = cash you can’t use

If you’re not sure, you’re not alone. Many small business owners pay “ballpark” estimates without knowing if they’re accurate.

To help, Lean Street Finance is offering complimentary 30-minute reviews for Indy Chamber Members. Together, we’ll look at:

  • How your federal tax payments compare to your prior year tax liability
  • Whether your cash flow is aligned with your payments
  • Steps to make sure you’re not overpaying or risking fees
